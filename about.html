How I Started

Without any knowledge of coding, about programming, without any knowledge of website creation, I needed to have some clear goal about what I want to learn. This goal was: I want to make a website. With research, firstly, I came to WordPress, free hosting and domain name. With WordPress I made couple of websites. Because those sites are tottaly free, they have subdomain .wordpress and some of them have advertisement for WordPress on their pages. Those websites are visible on google. Nine of my WordPress websites, that I made, are on first google page after search, without typing subdomain .wordpress. You only need to type, for example: informalno ucenje; mlekara mlecni zalogaj; teretana big cash; prirodnjacki centar sumarak; teretana drajfus; plesna skola mia; and you will see those sites on first google page.

My first goal was accomplished. Second goal was: I want to code site. By research I found free platforms for learning. I learned HTML5 and CSS3, Bootstrap, and basic JavaScript. In CodePen I made first sites by coding. But, CodePen is working platform and sites are not visible on google search. I realised that I need code editor and host. I downloaded Atom, free code editor and made files with HTML5 and CSS3. I chose 000webhost to host my sites and simply copy/pasted files on required space on my 000webhost profile. Three sites are on first google page, even they have free domain name and free hosting: informalno ucenje; prirodnjacki centar sumarak and dusan randjelovic portfolio. If you check w3 validator you may only find errors on sites where bootstrap file is incorporated. Obviously, BootStrap 4 has some bug.
